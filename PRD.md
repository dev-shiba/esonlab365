# ESONLAB DAYZ Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Overview
Goal: Transform the website into a "Brand Official Homepage" (ESONLAB DAYZ).
Format: Brand & Product-centric showcase, intended for both domestic users and overseas buyers.

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name**: 이손랩 데이즈 (ESONLAB DAYZ)
- **Primary Colors**: White (#FFFFFF), Blue (Main), Green (Sub/Secondary)
- **Aesthetic**: Apple-inspired 'Liquid Glass' design with glassmorphism, blur, and premium animations.

## Sitemap
1. **Home (Main)**: Visual hero section, brand philosophy teaser, trust indicators (partners), and certification marks.
2. **Brand (About)**: Brand story ("Care, Reconsidered. DAYZ"), focus on Korean ingredients and Spirulina research.
3. **Products**: Showcase of "SPIRUL365 GREEN HYDRO-LOCK CREAM", highlighting ingredients (Rice Bran Water 67%, Spirulina), clinical tests, 7-FREE policy.
4. **Contact**: Business inquiry via ESONLABDAYZ@GMAIL.COM.
